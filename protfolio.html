AgriRwanda

AgriRwanda is a comprehensive digital platform made by Titan Minds that empowers Rwandan farmers. It has various facilities such as weather patterns that help to stay alert of weather changes. It encompasses of a community for farmers, markeplaces and market conditions information. It also has various video tutorials and courses that are required for sustainable growth of farmers.

AgriRwanda is a special platform that is user-friendly, easy to navigate and comfortable to use. It has different features such as switching languages and light modes.
