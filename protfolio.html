MY ARTISTIC JOURNEY

Piercing Gaze

Piercing Gaze holds a depth of emotion and reflection. The gaze feels intense, almost like it's searching for something, or contemplating. The delicately rendered eyelashes, the way the iris catches the light, and the stark contrast of the shadows suggest an inner turmoil, but also a strength. It represents something more profound or hidden beneath the surface.
